# ClutchAI - Project Description

## Problem Statement Selected

Problem Statement 1: The Last-Minute Life Saver.

Traditional reminder apps tell users *when* something is due, but not whether
their real life leaves enough time to finish it. This creates false confidence
until a deadline becomes an emergency.

## Solution Overview

ClutchAI is an AI productivity companion that models the user's real capacity,
detects commitments at risk, explains why the risk exists, and actively creates
a recovery plan. Its central insight is that “three calendar days” may contain
only “11.5 productive hours” after sleep, college, work, travel, and other fixed
commitments are removed.

The product is designed to move users from anxiety to one credible next action.
It can simulate future choices, compress scope, protect focus blocks, dynamically
replan, and negotiate a tiny start when motivation is low.

## Key Features

1. Life-context and usable-time engine
2. Effective-days / effective-hours reveal
3. Dynamic success probability and risk classification
4. What-if future simulator
5. Agent-generated minimum-viable rescue plans
6. Contextual Gemini productivity coach
7. Consequence-aware prioritization
8. Life map with fixed and flexible commitments
9. XP, streak, shield, and progress feedback
10. Responsive, persistent, offline-capable demo experience

## Agentic Depth

ClutchAI does not merely produce text. It observes task size, progress, available
capacity, fixed commitments, consequences, and user resistance; chooses the most
important intervention; constructs an action plan; and updates the interface as
the user completes steps. A deterministic local engine keeps core actions
reliable while Gemini adds natural-language reasoning and negotiation.

## Impact

The product is useful for students, professionals, freelancers, and founders.
It reduces missed deadlines by correcting planning fallacies early and making
the next step small enough to begin.
