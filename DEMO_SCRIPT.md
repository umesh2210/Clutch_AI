# ClutchAI - 3 Minute Demo

## 0:00-0:25 - The problem

“Normal reminder apps say my project is due in three days. ClutchAI asks a more
honest question: how much usable time do I actually have?”

Show the hero reveal: **3 calendar days vs 11.5 usable hours**.

## 0:25-1:05 - Intelligence

Explain the probability ring, work remaining, and life constraints. Open
**Run what-if** and compare starting tonight with postponing until tomorrow.

Key line: “ClutchAI makes the cost of procrastination visible before I pay it.”

## 1:05-1:50 - Agentic action

Click **Build my rescue plan**. Show:

- minimum viable outcome;
- protected deep-work block;
- proof and testing;
- submission buffer.

Complete one step to trigger XP feedback.

Key line: “It does not remind me to solve the problem. It restructures the
problem into something I can finish.”

## 1:50-2:25 - Human resistance

Open **Ask Clutch** and type: “I am too tired to work.”

Show the negotiated 20-minute start. Mention that Gemini has the user's task,
deadline, capacity, and consequences as context.

## 2:25-2:50 - System view

Open **Life map** and briefly show college, travel, sleep, and peak-focus
constraints. Open **Progress** to show that the system rewards promises kept,
not hours spent looking busy.

## 2:50-3:00 - Close

“ClutchAI is the productivity coach that refuses to let a bad plan become a
missed deadline.”
