Vibe2Ship Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Who is eligible to participate? −
Vibe2Ship is open to all college students and working professionals across India and participation is completely free
of cost.
How can I register for Vibe2Ship? −
Participants must register through the Blockseblock platform by clicking the “Register Now” button in the first fold
of the event landing page. Only solo participation is allowed. Please check Resource Doc for reference
Who will receive certificates and prize money? −
Certificates of participation will be awarded to all participants who submit their solutions. The top 5 participants,
selected after the final presentation round, will receive cash prizes as per the prize pool along with a Certificate of
Merit.

Are there multiple problem statements in this hackathon? −
Yes, there will be 2 problem statements for participants to choose from. However, you can select and work on only 1
problem statement throughout the hackathon. Choose the one that best aligns with your interests, skills, and ideas.
The problem statements will be released on 22nd June, after the registration window closes.
Is there any specific tech stack or tool that participants must use? −
Yes. Google AI Studio will be the core tool for this hackathon and is expected to be used as part of your solution
development. Beyond that, participants are free to use any additional technologies, frameworks, or tools that best
support their project.
