General  Guidelines
 

   
General  Guidelines  
1.  Eligibility  
●  The  hackathon  is  open  to  all  college  students  and  working  professionals  across  
India.
 
 ●  All  participants  must  register  through  the  platform  before  the  registration  deadline  
(21
 
st
 
June
 
2026,
 
11:59
 
pm)
 
.
 
Late
 
entries
 
will
 
not
 
be
 
accepted.
 
 ●  Participation  is  strictly  individual  (solo).  Team  participation  will  not  be  permitted.  
2.  Mandatory  Tools  
●  Participants  are  required  to  use  Google  AI  Studio  as  part  of  their  solution  
development
 
process.
 
 ●  Additional  tools,  frameworks,  and  technologies  may  be  used  unless  otherwise  
specified
 
in
 
the
 
problem
 
statement.
 
3.  Originality  &  Fair  Use  
●  All  submissions  must  be  the  participant's  original  work.  
 ●  Use  of  open-source  libraries,  APIs,  and  publicly  available  resources  is  permitted  with  
proper
 
attribution.
 
 ●  Plagiarism,  copied  projects,  or  previously  submitted  hackathon  projects  may  result  in  
disqualification.
 
 ●  Organizers  reserve  the  right  to  conduct  originality  checks.  
4.  Prizes  &  Recognition  
●  Winners  will  be  announced  after  the  completion  of  the  final  presentation  round.  
 ●  Prize  money  shall  be  subject  to  applicable  taxes  and  statutory  deductions,  including  
TDS,
 
as
 
per
 
prevailing
 
Government
 
regulations.
 


   
 
5.
 
Code
 
of
 
Conduct
 
●  Participants  are  expected  to  maintain  professional  and  respectful  behavior  
throughout
 
the
 
event.
 
 ●  Any  form  of  harassment,  misconduct,  cheating,  or  abuse  may  result  in  immediate  
disqualification.
 
 ●  Organizers  reserve  the  right  to  remove  participants  who  violate  these  guidelines.  
6.  Intellectual  Property  
●  Participants  retain  ownership  of  their  submitted  projects.  
 ●  By  participating,  participants  grant  Coding  Ninjas  and  Google  for  Developers  the  right  
to
 
showcase
 
project
 
summaries,
 
screenshots,
 
demo
 
videos,
 
participant
 
names,
 
and
 
related
 
content
 
for
 
promotional,
 
educational,
 
and
 
community
 
purposes.
 
7.  Disqualification  
Organizers  reserve  the  right  to  disqualify  participants  for:  
●  Violation  of  hackathon  rules  ●  Plagiarism  or  fraudulent  submissions  ●  Misrepresentation  of  work  ●  Failure  to  meet  submission  requirements  ●  Unethical  conduct  
8.  Organizer  Rights  
●  Coding  Ninjas  and  Google  for  Developers  reserve  the  right  to  modify  timelines,  rules,  
evaluation
 
processes,
 
or
 
event
 
structure
 
if
 
required.
 
 ●  Any  such  changes  will  be  communicated  through  official  channels.  
9.  Final  Decision  
●  All  evaluation  decisions  made  by  the  judging  panel  shall  be  final  and  binding.  
 
●
 
No
 
disputes
 
or
 
appeals
 
regarding
 
judging
 
outcomes
 
will
 
be
 
entertained.
 


   
 
10.
 
Acceptance
 
of
 
Guidelines
 
●  Registration  for  the  hackathon  constitutes  acceptance  of  these  guidelines  and  any  
additional
 
instructions
 
communicated
 
by
 
the
 
organizers.
